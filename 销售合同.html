合同条款 CONTRACT TERMS & CONDITIONS

1. 合同生效：本合同自双方签字盖章之日起生效。 Contract Effective: This contract shall take effect upon signature and seal by both parties.

2. 交货条款：卖方应按照合同约定的时间、地点和方式交付货物。 Delivery Terms: The seller shall deliver the goods according to the agreed time, place and method.

3. 质量标准：货物质量应符合双方约定的标准或行业标准。 Quality Standard: The quality of goods shall meet the agreed standards or industry standards.

4. 付款条款：买方应按照合同约定的付款方式和时间支付货款。 Payment Terms: The buyer shall pay the purchase price according to the agreed payment method and time.

5. 验收条款：买方应在收到货物后3天内进行验收，如有异议应及时提出。 Acceptance Terms: The buyer shall inspect the goods within 3 days upon receipt and raise objections promptly if any.

6. 违约责任：任何一方违反合同约定，应承担相应的违约责任。 Liability for Breach: Any party breaching the contract shall bear corresponding liability.

7. 争议解决：因本合同产生的争议，双方应友好协商解决；协商不成的，提交有管辖权的法院诉讼解决。 Dispute Resolution: Disputes arising from this contract shall be settled through friendly negotiation; if failed, shall be submitted to the competent court.

8. 其他条款：本合同一式两份，双方各执一份，具有同等法律效力。 Other Terms: This contract is in duplicate, each party holds one copy, both have equal legal effect.
